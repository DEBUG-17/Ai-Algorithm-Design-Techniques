Algorithm Design Techniques

Objectives:

  • To learn design principles and concepts of algorithms.
  • To have a mathematical foundation in the analysis of algorithms.

importants:

  • Introduction to algorithm specification, pseudo code, time and space complexity, recursion tree method, and master method.
  • Searching and Sorting techniques like Binary Search, Merge Sort, and Quick Sort. Hashing methods including chaining and open addressing.
  • Greedy techniques (Knapsack Problem, Huffman Codes) and Dynamic Programming (Matrix Chain Multiplication, Longest Common Subsequence).
  • Graph algorithms such as BFS, DFS, Prim's, Kruskal's, and Dijkstra's algorithms.

Artificial Intelligence

Objectives:

  • To learn the basic concepts of AI principles and approaches.
  • To understand the building blocks of AI.

importants:

  • Introduction to AI, Turing Test, Intelligent Agents, and applications of AI.
  • Problem-solving techniques like BFS, DFS, A*, and Min-Max algorithms.
  • Knowledge Representation including Predicate Logic, Semantic Nets, and Conceptual Graphs.
  • Dealing with uncertainty, Bayesian Inference, and basics of NLP.
